Daniel Greenfeld (cartwheelweb.com) bio
Daniel Greenfeld is the VP of development at cartwheelweb.com and also works as a freelance consultant for and revsys.com. Working with the chairman of the Python Software Foundation he helped author a series of classes on Python 3 for horeillyschool.com. He is a co-founder of djangopackages.com and some of his other projects include django-uni-form.rtfd.org, and the the infamous http://pypi.python.org/pypi/that package. He has been a core contributor to pinaxproject.com. His speaking history is available at http://lanyrd.com/people/pydanny/sessions/ and he blogs at http://pydanny.blogspot.com.
